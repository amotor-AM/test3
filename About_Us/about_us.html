Why were we created?

In an ever increasingly connected world plant maintenence seems to be stuck in the middle ages. We set out to solve this. Through the use of our automated plant watering service we empower users to become plant experts. Our system does all the hard work for you. All you have to do is set up the app and we will do the rest. You will automatically be notified at the optimal watering times for each of your plants. At Water My Plants we believe everybody can become plant experts.
